about us

MEAEC is approved by the AICTE ,new delhi and affiliated to APJ abdul Kalam technological university. MEA engineering collage is first NAAC accredited self-financing engineering collage in malappuram district

MEA Engineering College, the first NAAC accredited self-financing engineering college in Malappuram district, is committed to provide excellent and value-based education with a flair for ethics and professionalism. The College is located amidst panoramic natural beauty over-looking the hills and valleys at Nellikunnu, near Perinthalmanna – a city of various educational institutions- on the Perinthalmanna- Melattur road. The College is easily accessible by road, rail and air. The College is governed by the MEA sponsored by the “Samastha Kerala Jammiyyathul Ulama”, a society instituted by top class intellectuals of the Muslim community.
